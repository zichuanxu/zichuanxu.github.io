Zichuan Xu

How do agents learn to coordinate?

I am a master's student at The University of Osaka researching multi-agent learning, emergent communication, and practical AI systems.

Research should hold up beyond a shared training run.

My work asks how independently trained agents can create, interpret, and adapt communication protocols when their partners or environments change.

I study in the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Chuan Xiao.

Research
Multi-agent reinforcement learning, emergent communication, and cross-play robustness.
Engineering
Agent systems, efficient inference, backend services, and practical evaluation infrastructure.

The questions I keep returning to.

Emergence

What makes a learned signal become useful, stable, and interpretable?

Transfer

Evaluation
